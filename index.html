Past Projects

Bringing Miel Milsantos to life; A Live2D project

Creating Miel was a week long project where I challenged myself to learn the process of bringing a Live2D Model to life in a week, and finish the week with a clear understanding of the technical and creative skill behind it.

Live 2D is an animation technique used to animate static images; it involves separating the image into parts and animating each part accordingly This enables characters to move in 2.5D movement, while maintaining original illustration.

In a week, I underwent all phases of sketching, illustrating, cutting and finally animating my model. Miel was a journey meant to show that I take initiative with learning things, adapting and rising to new challenges.

To learn Live2D animation software and finish a project on it was an enjoyable journey that sprouted the passion for animating in Live2D. As of today, Miel is an integral part of my weekend D&D sessions.

Live2D can be used with real-time motion capture to track movements such as head movements, eye movements, and perform lip syncing for real-time applications. I had the idea of bringing her to life from the need for a more creative and visual approach to a hobby of mine, creating a more immersive and engaging experience and bringing a new immersive aspect to our fantasy world.
